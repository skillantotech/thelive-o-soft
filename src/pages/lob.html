Line of Business



Liveosoft offers comprehensive, end-to-end solutions for the agriculture industry, focusing on modernizing farming practices and maximizing productivity. From plantation management and biotech innovations to smart farming techniques and sustainable resource use, we empower farmers, agribusinesses, and institutions to optimize their operations. Our diverse expertise extends to government institutions, FPOs, NGOs, and agribusiness companies, providing customized tools for efficient farm management, crop monitoring, and international exports. Liveosoft drives innovation through advanced R&D, contract farming solutions, and organic farming practices, fostering a future-ready agriculture ecosystem.

Exports and Packers

Liveosoft offers solutions for exporters and packers, ensuring that agricultural products meet international quality standards, streamlining packaging processes, and optimizing supply chain logistics for global distribution.

We promote organic farming practices by providing tools and resources to manage organic crops, ensure certification compliance, and connect farmers with niche markets for organic produce.

Plantation and Farm

Liveosoft provides end-to-end solutions for managing plantations and farms, enhancing productivity through smart farming techniques.
