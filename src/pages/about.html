About Us

Company History

Liveosoft was founded in 2024 with the vision of transforming the livestock industry in India. Established by industry pioneers Mr. X, Mr. Y, and Mr. Z, our company was born out of a shared passion for leveraging technology to enhance livestock farming practices. Since our inception, Liveosoft has rapidly grown into a trusted name in the livestock sector, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and excellence.

Our Mission

Our mission at Liveosoft is to lead the livestock industry into a new era of efficiency, sustainability, and growth. We aim to empower farmers, producers, and businesses by providing access to top-quality livestock, advanced production techniques, and streamlined supply chain solutions.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the foremost agritech company in the region, driving positive change in the agricultural landscape through innovation, ethical practices, and a deep commitment to the welfare of animals and the environment.
